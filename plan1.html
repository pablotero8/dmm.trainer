Program Overview

Designed for people who want to improve overall health, general fitness, and performance without the need to compete. This 8-week program is perfect for those looking to build a solid fitness foundation with personalized guidance and ongoing support.

What's Included

  • Personalized online training program
  • Improved general strength, endurance, and mobility
  • Adapted to your level, equipment, and available time
  • Weekly follow-up and adjustments throughout 8 weeks

Program Structure

The Performance Base program is structured to progressively build your fitness level over the 8-week period:

  • Week 1-2: Assessment and foundation building
  • Week 3-4: Progressive strength development
  • Week 5-6: Endurance and capacity building
  • Week 7-8: Performance consolidation and advanced techniques

Ideal For

Active people, recreational players, or athletes who want to feel stronger, faster, and with fewer aches. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or looking to elevate your current level, this program provides the perfect balance of challenge and sustainability.

How It Works

  • Initial consultation to understand your goals and current fitness level
  • Customized training program sent to you every week
  • Video demonstrations for all exercises
  • Weekly check-ins to track progress and make adjustments
  • Direct access for questions and clarifications

Expected Results

By the end of the 8 weeks, you can expect:

  • Increased strength and muscle tone
  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Better mobility and flexibility
  • Reduced pain and discomfort from daily activities
  • Increased energy and confidence
  • Sustainable habits for long-term fitness
